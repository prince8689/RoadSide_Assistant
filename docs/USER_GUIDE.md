# RoadAssist — User Guide
## For Vehicle Owners

---

## Getting Started

### 1. Create Your Account
- Go to https://your-app.vercel.app
- Click "Create Account"
- Select "I need help" (Vehicle Owner)
- Fill in your details:
  - Full Name
  - Email Address
  - Phone Number
  - Password (min 8 chars, 1 uppercase, 1 number)
- Click "Register"
- You will be redirected to your dashboard

### 2. Add Your Vehicle
- Go to "My Vehicles" from sidebar
- Click "Add Vehicle"
- Fill in details:
  - Make (e.g. Honda)
  - Model (e.g. City)
  - Year (e.g. 2022)
  - License Plate (e.g. DL01AB1234)
  - Fuel Type
  - Color
- Click Save
- You can add multiple vehicles

## Requesting Roadside Assistance

### Step 1 — Select Service Type
Choose from 5 services:
| Service | Description | Est. Price |
|---------|-------------|------------|
| Breakdown Repair | On-site diagnosis | ₹400+ |
| Towing | Vehicle tow to garage | ₹500+ |
| Battery Jump-start | Jump start dead battery | ₹299+ |
| Flat Tire Repair | Tire change or repair | ₹350+ |
| Fuel Delivery | Fuel delivery to location | ₹250+ |

### Step 2 — Select Your Vehicle
- Choose which vehicle needs help
- Verify vehicle details are correct

### Step 3 — Confirm Your Location
- Your current location is auto-detected
- Drag the map marker to adjust if needed
- Enter your address in the text box
- Add description of the problem (optional)

### Step 4 — Submit Request
- Review summary (service, vehicle, location, price)
- Click "Confirm Request"
- You will be redirected to the tracking page

## Tracking Your Mechanic

After submitting a request:

### Status Updates
| Status | What it means |
|--------|--------------|
| 🟡 Pending | Finding a mechanic near you |
| 🔵 Accepted | Mechanic found and confirmed |
| 🟣 En Route | Mechanic is heading to you |
| 🔵 Arrived | Mechanic reached your location |
| 🟠 In Progress | Work is being done |
| 🟢 Completed | Service done successfully |

### Live Map
- Your location shown with blue marker
- Mechanic location shown with orange marker
- Mechanic marker moves in real time

### Mechanic Details
- Name and business name
- Star rating and experience
- Phone number (click to call)

### Cancel Request
- You can cancel if status is Pending or Accepted
- Click "Cancel Request" → enter reason → confirm

## After Service

### Submit Review
- After service is completed
- Go to My Requests → find completed request
- Click "Leave Review"
- Select star rating (1-5)
- Write a comment (optional)
- Click Submit

### View Service History
- Go to "My Requests" from sidebar
- Filter by: All / Active / Completed / Cancelled
- Click any request to see full details

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How long does it take to find a mechanic?**
A: Usually within 2-5 minutes if mechanics are available nearby.

**Q: Can I track the mechanic on the map?**
A: Yes! Once a mechanic accepts your request, you can see their live location on the map.

**Q: What if no mechanic accepts my request?**
A: Your request stays active for 30 minutes. You can cancel and re-request if needed.

**Q: Is payment done through the app?**
A: Currently payment is done directly to the mechanic after service. Online payment will be available in a future update.

**Q: Can I cancel my request?**
A: Yes, you can cancel when status is Pending or Accepted. Once mechanic has started work, cancellation is not available.
