# RoadAssist — Mechanic / Partner Guide

---

## Getting Started

### 1. Register as a Partner
- Go to https://your-app.vercel.app
- Click "Create Account"
- Select "I am a mechanic" (Service Partner)
- Fill in your details and register

### 2. Complete Your Profile
After registration, complete your service profile:
- Business Name
- Years of Experience
- Specializations (select all that apply):
  - Breakdown Repair
  - Towing
  - Battery Jump-start
  - Flat Tire Repair
  - Fuel Delivery
- Documents:
  - Driving License Number
  - Aadhar Card Number
  - Service Certificate

### 3. Wait for Verification
- Admin reviews your profile within 24 hours
- You will receive a notification when verified
- After verification you can start accepting requests

---

## Receiving Service Requests

### Go Online
- Click the availability toggle in the top navbar
- Toggle shows 🟢 Available when online
- You will only receive requests when Available

### New Request Alert
- When a user nearby requests help:
  - Request card appears on your dashboard instantly
  - Toast notification shown
  - You can see: Service type, distance, estimated price

### Accept or Reject
- Click ✅ ACCEPT to take the job
- Click ❌ REJECT to pass (request stays for other mechanics)
- You have limited time to respond

---

## Managing Active Jobs

### After Accepting
- You are redirected to Active Job page
- Map shows user's breakdown location
- Update your status at each step:

| Button | Status | When to click |
|--------|--------|---------------|
| 🚗 I'm On My Way | En Route | When you start driving |
| 📍 I've Arrived | Arrived | When you reach location |
| 🔧 Start Work | In Progress | When you begin repairs |
| ✅ Mark Complete | Completed | When job is done |

### Live Location Sharing
- Your location automatically shares when job is active
- User can see you moving on their map
- Location updates every 5 seconds
- Stops automatically when job is completed

### Call Customer
- Click "Call Customer" button for their number
- Available on Active Job page

## Earnings

### View Earnings
- Go to "Earnings" from sidebar
- See total earnings and per-job breakdown
- Filter by: All / This Week / This Month

### Payment
- Collect payment directly from customer after service
- Online payment feature coming in future update

---

## Reviews

- Go to "Reviews" from sidebar
- See all customer reviews
- Rating breakdown (1★ to 5★)
- Higher rating = more requests from nearby users

---

## Tips for Success
- Stay online during peak hours (9AM-9PM)
- Accept requests quickly (faster response = better ranking)
- Maintain rating above 4.0 for priority listing
- Keep your location updated for accurate distance
- Update status promptly at each step

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I get more requests?**
A: Stay online, maintain high rating, and accept requests quickly.

**Q: What if I can't complete a job after accepting?**
A: Contact the customer via phone. Admin can help resolve disputes.

**Q: How is my rating calculated?**
A: Average of all star ratings from completed jobs.

**Q: When will I get verified?**
A: Admin verifies profiles within 24 hours on business days.
